A Vision Built on Engineering Excellence

Canadian Engineering Consultant is a multidisciplinary engineering consultancy firm established in 1996, delivering comprehensive engineering, planning, architectural, structural, and MEP design solutions across the region.

Headquartered in the UAE, the firm operates across the GCC and maintains strong relationships with government entities, private developers, utilities, consultants, and contractors — earning a reputation as a trusted engineering and design partner.

From concept and planning to design and project completion, every solution we deliver reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and efficiency.
